QR Code Scanner

 At Uber, I worked on a QR code scanner to transfer authenticated user sessions across devices, built in React/Redux. For example, if a user is logged in on their phone and wishes to log in via desktop, they can simple scan the QR code, confirm on their phone, and log in on their computer. WhatsApp has a similar implementation.

Haiti Water Filters

 Over spring break in my senior year of highschool, I traveled to Haiti with around twenty other seniors and two teachers. We stayed in a compound near Port Au Prince and installed water filters for the local community. It was a great experience to get away from our phones and live in the moment while learning about a different. The trip was in cooperation with the non-profit organization Poured Out.
