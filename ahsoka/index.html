The Best Animal in the Neighborhood

Ahsoka (pronounced ahh-SO-ca) is a hound and beagle mix. She howls at anyone who approaches our house, but once she smells your shoes, she will love you!

Give Her a Treat

Part Beagle

Her large, flappy ears look like mini solar panels on her head. Add her cute face and ability to learn tricks like roll over, wait, and jump, and you have plenty of beagle characteristics.

Part Hound

If you hear howling from the neighbor's house, it isn't a wolf! This dog loves to utilize her vocal cords when meeting new guests, but don't worry... it's only a precautionary measure done out of love for the family.

All Fun

In her spare time, Ahsoka enjoys going on walks, chasing rodents(raccoons, squirrels, and chipmunks), and eating. Guided by feral instincts, she roams outdoors and defends the lawn from the evil people walking by.
